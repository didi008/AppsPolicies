# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 22, 2024** (Note: adjust date if needed)

This Privacy Policy describes how **Learning Quiz: Flags** (the "Application") handles your information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed to be **100% offline**. 

- **No Personal Data Collection:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or location).
- **No Tracking:** We do not use any tracking technologies, cookies, or analytics services to monitor your usage of the Application.
- **No Account Required:** You do not need to create an account to use the Application.

## 2. Permissions

The Application does not request any sensitive permissions on your device (such as access to your camera, contacts, or microphone).

## 3. Third-Party Services

The Application uses flag data and images sourced from **FlagCDN** (flagcdn.com). These resources are used solely for the educational purpose of the quiz. No user data is shared with FlagCDN or any other third-party providers.

## 4. Data Security

Since the Application does not collect or transmit any data, your information remains entirely on your device. We do not have access to any of your data.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The Application is safe for all ages. It does not collect any personal information from children or any other users.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
