# Privacy Policy for Fun Dart Score (FDS)

**Effective Date:** July 08, 2026

Kirbytte ("we", "our", or "us") operates the **Fun Dart Score (FDS)** mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**Fun Dart Score (FDS)** is designed with privacy in mind. We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data that can identify you.

### Local Storage only
Any information you enter into the App, such as:
- Player names
- Game scores and statistics
- App settings (sound preferences)

is stored **locally on your device** using an internal database (Room). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible by us or any third party.

## 2. Permissions

The App may require the following standard permissions to function correctly:
- **Audio/Media**: To play victory and podium sounds during the game.
- **Vibration**: To provide haptic feedback (if enabled by your device settings).

We do **not** request access to sensitive information such as your location, contacts, camera, or microphone.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App does **not** use any third-party services that may collect information used to identify you, such as:
- No Advertising networks (Ads)
- No Analytics tools (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- No Cloud synchronization or social media logins

## 4. Security

The security of your data is important to us. Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on the security measures of your mobile operating system. If you delete the App, all locally stored data (players and stats) will be permanently removed.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Our App is safe for users of all ages, including children. Because the App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data and has no internet communication capabilities, it is fully compliant with children's privacy regulations (such as COPPA and GDPR). We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from anyone.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
